— Our Process —

Step 1: Consultation. In our initial meeting, we will discuss overall project details and go into depth about functionality and appearance. This is where we will discuss the cost and timeline of each part of the project.

Step 2: Prototype. Following the notes from Step 1, we will create a sample of your project by the agreed upon time. This prototype will include the functionality and general layout that was requested.

Step 3: Feedback. We present our sample to you and take note of any changes you'd like to see. Tell us everything you want changed and we'll narrow down exactly what we can do for you.

Step 4: Edit and Optimize. We take the notes from all our meetings and polish up the project to reflect your vision. Customer satisfaction is a top priority so will work diligently to produce a high end product.

Step 5: Delivery and Payment. Once we can deliver a product that matches or exceeds your expectations, we will deliver the project back to you in the agreed amount of time. This will include a thorough explanation of all aspects of your project
